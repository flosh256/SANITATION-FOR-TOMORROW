INTRODUCTION AND IMPORTANCE OF SANITATION SAFETY

Commitment to safe Sanitation Practices

Ensuring a healthy and safe environment through proper sanitation practices and worker protection

WHY SANITATION SAFETY MATTERS

It increases the risks of poor sanitation such as disease spread,environmental contamination,worker injury.

HYGIENE PRACTICES

Hand washing protocols

When to wash:Before eating, after using the toilets,after handling waste

How to wash: Detailed step(wet,soap,lather for 20 seconds,scrub all areas,rinse dry)

WASTE HANDLING AND DISPOSAL SAFETY

Waste classification: Information on distinguishing between general waste, infectious waste and sharps

sharps safety: Never reach into a waste container Use dustpans to move sharp objects like needles

Infectios waste:Do not compactinfectious waste. Use leak-proof,puncture-resistant containers. Keep infectious waste separate from regular trash

BENEFITS OF SANITATION SAFETY

It increases workers efficiency and productivity

It reduces the risk of disease spread such as cholera,typhoid

It reduces water contamination

It increases healthy in different communities
